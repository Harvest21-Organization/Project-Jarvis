Harvest21 is a unified missionary engagement platform designed to give missionaries highly customizable landing pages where they can post updates, accept donations, connect with followers, and collaborate with mission agencies, sending churches, and colleges. The product enhances donor engagement, boosts transparency, and centralizes communication for all stakeholders. It features user authentication, content uploads (video, documents, photos), email/text notifications, account management tools, and powerful discovery and filtering tools for the missionary ecosystem. The primary users are missionaries, donors, churches, mission agencies, college administrators, and prospective supporters. The key benefits include streamlined missionary visibility, secure donation processing, and rich, data-driven insights for energy-efficient outreach.
