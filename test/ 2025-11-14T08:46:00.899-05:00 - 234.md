## ‚ú® tl;dr

Harvest 21 is a multifaceted platform designed to connect missionaries, mission agencies, churches, colleges, and individual supporters in a centralized and highly interactive web/app experience. The goal is to empower missionaries by offering customizable landing pages, while also providing agencies and churches with tools to highlight, monitor, and engage with support efforts. The platform features follow functionality, donations (one-time and recurring), updates, live events, prayer walls, and robust search and sorting capabilities. Users can create accounts, follow missionaries, customize notifications, and view impact metrics. Key users include missionaries, individual donors/supporters, mission agencies, churches, colleges, and administrative staff.

- Monitoring via CloudWatch, Models auto-scale via Fargate triggers
